# SQL Queries
This folder contains SQL queries used for data exploration and business analysis in the Super Store Sales Analysis project.

## Queries Included
1. Total Sales
2. Sales by Category
3. Sales by Region
4. Sales by Segment
5. Total Orders
6. Total Profit
7. Profit by Category
8. Discount vs Profit Analysis
9. Top States by Sales
10. Top Cities by Profit
11. Sales By Sub-Category
12. Profit by Region
13. Quantity by Category
14. Average Discount by Category
15. Top Profitable Sub-Categories
16. Loss-Making Sub-Categories
17. High Discount Orders
18. High Sales but Low Profit
19. Profit Margin by Category
20. Category Performance

These SQL queries provide valuable business insights into sales performance, profitability, discounts, regional trends, product categories, and overall business performance.
